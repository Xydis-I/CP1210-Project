Welcome To Trivia Dungeon!

In Trivia Dungeon the player is an adventurer well versed in the art word smithing.
The player will have to used the knowledge that they have gained over the years from playing games
to smite the enemies that block their escape from the Trivia Dungeon!




This is a version of the game that has been made without the use of PixiJS.
